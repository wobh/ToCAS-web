Tales of Clark Ashton Smith

Introduction

Clark Ashton Smith (1893–1961) was an author of fantastical weird fiction, who, with H.P. Lovecraft and Robert E. Howard, pioneered modern fantasy, science-fiction, and horror in the pulp era. He invented several settings and wrote many stories in each. I have collected a few of them in each setting here.
